Hi!  I'm a PhD candidate in the mathematics department at the University of Oregon where I am supervised by Dev Sinha.  My research interests are in topological data analysis and applied topology, specifically generalizations of the Mapper algorithm and it's applications.

I earned my bachelor of science degree in mathematics at North Dakota State University (where I was advised by Seppi Dorfmeister).
